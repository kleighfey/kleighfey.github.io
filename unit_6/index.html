This months bundles

Bundles + Bolts feature carefully curated collections of the best quilting and knit fabric. Three makers, three collections!

Bundle 1

Amanda Iversen-Whittle's latest collection is made up of a delicate floral, monochromatic blenders, and soft pastel solids. Made for the garden dreamer, it combines rich plums and mustards with subdued pinks and mints. This collection includes two buttery soft fabrics from Art Gallery Fabrics, two organic Cloud9 solids, and geometric prints from Riley Blake.

Learn More

Bundle 2

A bright boho summer party on the patio is the inspiration behind April Riley Tate's fabric collection. She contrasts sun drenched florals and southwestern-inspired graphic prints and softens this edgy palette with warm rose and burnished golds. This cheerful combination of large-scale and small print fabrics from Art Gallery Fabrics, Moda, and Riley Blake are your summer glamping inspiration!

Learn More

Bundle 3

Boys will be boys, but mommas make their quilts! Lilo's most recent bundle features arrows, plaids, and a walk through a stick wood forest that will inspire a navy and teal adventure for the whole family. Three of the fabrics are from the soft collections of Art Gallery Fabrics, two are rich tone on tone marbles from Riley Blake, and a dark/light grid round out this ready to play bundle.
